Washington D.C.
District of Columbia - US

Washington is the capital city of the USA, it’s located in the District of Columbia, a federal district not a state, and it’s the home of about 700 000 citizens. This city was founded after the american revolution, and it’s named after the first president of the federation George Washington and one of the founding fathers of the nation. In the centre of the city there’s the Nationall Mall, the biggest park of the city where sometimes people organize political protest, concerts, festival and the presidential settlement.

  • Washington landmarks

  • White house
  • Smithsonian national history museum
  • Lincoln memorial
  • Washington memorial
  • Jefferson memoria
  • National art gallery

  • Culture

This citiy is the host of various presidential memorial, national museum and government istitution, it’s a very popular destination for school trips. Washington has also a different side of culture the music culture, in fact the city has its own genre of music the so called “go-go” a percussion driven type of R&B. The city is also the host of a parody basketball team, the Washington Generals, which the famous Harlem Globetrotters play in some exibition/parosy games over the course of the year. The city public transportation and the metro bus system is organized by the WMATA, washington metropolitan area transit authority.This metropolitan system is the second most used in the country.


In the city there are also various active sport teams:

  • Washington Wizards (basketball)
  • Washington Redskins (football)
  • Washington Nationals (baseball)
  • Washington Capitals (hockey)

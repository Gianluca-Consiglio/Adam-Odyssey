Portland
Oregon - U.S.

Portland is a city of 650 000 people located in the state of Oregon, in the north west of the US facing the Pacific Ocean. The origin of the name derives from the homonymous city of maine on the east coast that is because people emigrated from the east to the west during the XIX century.

  • Culture

The city i salso known as “the city of roses” for the famous Portland Rose Festival, during this event in the city is organized a Grand Floral Parade in which floral carts parade through the streets. Another event is the Waterfront Blues festival, the second most important blues festival of the US, that is held on the banks of the river Willamette. In the city there only one important sport franchise the Portland Trial Blazers a basketball team that plays in the Moda Center. Portland is a multicultural city and that is shown in the city’s census in which the so called minorities make up more than 30% of the 650 000 citizens.

The different types of ethnicities are shown in the various dishes that you can find in the city, here’s a list:

  • Doughnuts
  • Chocolate chip cookies
  • Foie gras profiterols

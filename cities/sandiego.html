San Diego
California - U.S.

San Diego is a city of 3.0 million citizens located in the southern part of California, on the west coast of the US facing the Pacific Ocean. It’s the second most populated city in California after LA. The city is most known for the marines’ and navy’s bases but is also a well known touristic destination, for example the are three different amusement park in San Diego: Seaworld, San Diego Zoo and the Balboa Park.

  • San Diego landmarks

  • Balboa park
  • Zoo
  • Seaworld
  • USS Midway museum
  • La jolla cove
  • Seaport village

  • Culture

The city is a mix of latino and american culture, typical of the cities near the border with Mexico. In the city during the year are organized different events such as the San Diego Pride, Del Mar Fair (an edgy festival) and the Comic-Con the biggest conference of comics in the world. Other interesting activities that you can experience in San Diego are the long walks that you can do at the beach, the two most important are the ocean beach and the pacific beach, and surfing.

In the the city there are only two professional teams in the american major leageus:

  • San Diego Padres (baseball)
  • San Diego Chargers (football)

Being close to the border with Mexico the city food industry is influenced by spanish cousine, here’s a list of the dishes of San Diego:

  • Carne Asada
  • Taco
  • Burrito
  • Ceviche

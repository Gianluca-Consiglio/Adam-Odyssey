New York City
New York - U.S.

New York City is located in the state of New York on the east cost facing the atlantic ocean, it’s formed by five districts (Bronx, Mahattan, Queens, Brooklyn, Staten Island). Almost 8.7 millions people live here, and it’s considered as the most important city of the east coast. It’s is “The Big Apple”, which in italian is translated to “La Grande Mela”, because during the 1800’s the north of the USA, and especially N.Y., was seen as the more developed part of the country because the U.S.A. was compared to a tree as a metaphor, in which the southerner states where the trunk and the roots of the economy while the northerners states were the “fruits” of the economy and the more developed sector of the economy.

  • Culture

New York is a cauldron of different ethnicities, religions , languages (such as italian, spanish, english of course etc…). It is known to be very chaotic at times, traffic jams are very frequent even though a large part of citizens don’t own a car. The most popular mean of trasportation is by far is the famous NY underground, which is the most extensive metropolitan system in the world, another alternative is the use of the classic yellow taxi which you can find literally in every street of the city. Every year since 1970, usually in autumn, the city is the host of the most partecipated marathon in the world, also in the city you can follow different sport franchises such as:

  • New York Knicks (basketball)
  • Brooklyn Nets (basketball)
  • New York Giants (football)
  • New York Jets (football)
  • New York Mets (baseball)
  • New York Yankees (baseball)

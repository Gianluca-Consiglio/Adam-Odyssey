Los Angeles
California - U.S.

Los Angeles is a city in the state of California in the west coast of the US facing the Pacific Ocean, it has a population of almost 4 million people, the county of LA is the third largest metropolitan economy of the world. Some studies have found that if every single human of the Earth lined up shoulder to shoulder they would fit in the area of the city which is roughly around 3000 squared kilometers. During the XIX century the city became the centre of show business of the US and the western world, in the end of the 800’s the famous city of Hollywood was founded by a certain Hobart Whitley, who’s considered the father of Hollywood, near LA; with the advent of the film industry the small ranch of Hollywood became the Mecca of American cinema where most of the actors and producers moved permanently.

  • Culture

The traditional cultures of LA are the latino and indigenous ones, with the arrival of the 20th century and the influence of the film industry the culture became more and more global, a mix of different culture from aroun the globe. LA is a city that thanks to philantropist and benefactors, has several museums and important places in term of elitist culture, such as painting and sculpture. During the last decade the street culture has spread in the city and led to the emergence of hipster neighborhoods an example is Melrose Aveue, which is located between Beverly Hills and Santa Monica, where you can go on a shopping spree or you can go eat at one of the resturants with ethnic cousine. Another important aspect of Los Angeles is the beach, Santa Monica , and the different activities and sport that you can perform an example are the bodybuilders of Muscle beach which is an outdoor gym.
